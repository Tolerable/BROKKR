This project proposes a strategic collaboration between cannabis breeder Bryan Rousey and hip hop artist Jayy Wick to develop, produce, launch, and commercialize an exclusive artist-branded cannabis strain - timed with MJBizCon 2026 (December 1-4 in Las Vegas).
